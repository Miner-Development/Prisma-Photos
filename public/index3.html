About Us

Open your eyes and indulge in Colorado's first and only THC infused dehydrated fruit edible – Forbidden Fruit from Giving Tree Kitchens!

Available in six decadent dehydrated varieties — Pineapple, Kiwi, Mango, Apricot, Cinnamon Apple, and Papaya!
